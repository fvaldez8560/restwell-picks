Privacy policy

This privacy policy explains what information RestWell picks ("we", "us", or "our") collects when you visit this website, how we use it, and the choices you have. We keep this simple on purpose: we run a small content site that recommends mattresses, and we collect as little as possible.

The short version: we don't sell your personal information. We use basic analytics to understand which pages are helpful, and some of our outbound links are affiliate links that may earn us a commission at no extra cost to you.

Information we collect

We may collect the following, depending on how you use the site:

  • Usage data: anonymous or aggregated information such as the pages you visit, your approximate region, browser type, and how you arrived at the site. This is typically gathered through analytics tools.
  • Device and log data: standard information your browser sends automatically, such as IP address and referring page, which may be logged by our hosting provider.
  • Information you choose to give us: for example, if you email us a question, we'll have your email address and whatever you include in your message.

How we use information

  • To understand which content is useful and improve our recommendations.
  • To keep the site secure, functioning, and free of abuse.
  • To respond to questions or messages you send us.

Affiliate links

Some links on this site are affiliate links. If you click one and make a purchase, the retailer may pay us a commission at no additional cost to you. These links may set a cookie in your browser so the retailer can attribute the sale to us. We only recommend products we believe are genuinely worth considering, and a partnership never changes our honest opinion or rating.

Cookies and analytics

Cookies are small files stored in your browser. We (and third-party services such as analytics providers and the retailers we link to) may use cookies to remember preferences, measure traffic, and attribute affiliate referrals. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings; disabling them won't prevent you from reading the site, though some features may behave differently.

Third-party links and services

When you click through to a retailer or any other external site, that site's own privacy policy applies. We don't control and aren't responsible for the content or privacy practices of sites we link to. We also rely on third-party providers (for hosting, fonts, and analytics) who process limited technical data on our behalf.

Children's privacy

This site is intended for adults making household purchasing decisions. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Your choices

You can browse this site without giving us any personal information. You may adjust cookie settings in your browser, opt out of many analytics cookies, and contact us to ask what information we may hold about you or to request its deletion.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "last updated" date above. Significant changes may be noted more prominently on the site.
